ABOUT HELLY LUV

Helan Abdulla (born November 16, 1988), better known by her stage name Helly Luv,[1] is a Kurdish-Finnish singer, dancer, choreographer, actress and model who was born in Urmia, Iran. Her career began by uploading cover videos on social media websites like Myspace and YouTube. Helly Luv has since starred in movies, music videos and has released her own material. Gaining popularity from her 2013 single "Risk It All", Helly Luv has recently signed to G2 Music Group.In 2014, she appeared in her first theatrical feature film, Mardan.
