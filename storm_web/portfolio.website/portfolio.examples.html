My Portfolio

Original Cyberpunk Poster

Created as a commentary of how popular cyberpunk movies share similar female paradigms and themes as those of the 1950's. I appropriated Richard Hamilton's "Just What Is It That Makes Today's Homes So Different, So Appealing" because it uses cultural references of the 1950's and gender stereotypes, in a way that reminded me of cyberpunk movies.

Dream Concert Website

In my Coding For The Web class, we were challenged to design a imaginary concert concept, and build a website premoting it, using HTML5 and CSS. My concert was a group powerful women from all around the world. These artist use their music for social and political reform and empowerment, and together they form their own cultural revolution.

Modern Damask Print

As a design exercise, I created this original damask print, using illustrator. I was inspired by the recent women's marches around the world, and wanted to remix the original "Rosie the Riveter," to represent women of different ethnicities and races, coming together as a force of nature.
